========================================
Yi-1.5
========================================

- **Context Length:** 4096
- **Model Name:** Yi-1.5
- **Languages:** en, zh
- **Abilities:** generate
- **Description:** Yi-1.5 is an upgraded version of Yi. It is continuously pre-trained on Yi with a high-quality corpus of 500B tokens and fine-tuned on 3M diverse fine-tuning samples.
